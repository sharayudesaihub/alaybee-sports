About Us

ALAYBEE SHOP is an exclusive online sports shop established by Alaybee Enterprises Pvt. Ltd. to provide the customers with the best quality sports equipment & services. Alaybee being into sports equipment manufacturing since 66 years and has a rich experience of 3 generations with continued success as a leader.

Mohammad A. Baker

Alaybee has been awarded No. 1 since 2004-05 for Highest Exports of Athletics Equipment from India by SGEPC. ALAYBEE SHOP is established to offer customers better products & services, by giving following benefits: Complete range of Alaybee Sports Equipment online, so customer can select the best sports product that is most suitable for them by comparing their features and prices. Product will be delivered directly from the manufacturer, which means always the latest production. Customer can clear their queries on products or terms & conditions by directly communicating with the manufacturer i.e. clear communication.

Authenticity of the product as it will be always direct from Alaybee factory. Easy order management through history, wish-list etc. Secure payment through reliable payment gateway. Access to latest products more easily. Fast, safe & secure shipping (all over India). Better prices and much more……
